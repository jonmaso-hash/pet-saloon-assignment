The Fur-legged Friend Saloon in Derry Maine is proud to offer a range of grooming services... This shop was started one day when I met a pup named Pele (short for Pelegrosa) in Tecate, Mexico, on my ex girlfriend's dad's property. Little Pele was sooooooo cute. Definely the cutest out of her brothers and sisters. So I started feeding her and her family, then snatached the pretty baby and brought into the house (don't worry, her mom and dad approved of me). I brought Pele back to her family often, so she wouldn't get home sick. Thus, began my love for animals, specifially dogs. Prior, I was a cat guy. Pele taught me there's more to life than cute meows. So we opened our hearts and a shop to meet other four-legged friends.
